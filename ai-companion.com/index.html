What is Fae?

Intelligence that stays with you,
not in someone's data centre.

Fae is a private AI assistant that runs entirely on your own device — no internet required, no cloud, no account, no logs. Your conversations never leave your machine. Built on Apple Silicon with open-source models, Fae is free to use and free to inspect. It exists because the best AI shouldn't require trusting a company with your most private thoughts.
