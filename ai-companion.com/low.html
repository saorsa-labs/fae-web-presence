How she works

You shouldn't have to edit yourself
to use an AI.

There's a small tax you pay every time you use any other AI. A half-second pause before you type, where you decide what version of the truth to share. What to leave out. How to phrase it so it feels less exposing. Fae is the only one where that pause just... goes.

She is closed, so you can be open.

From making your budget work to helping your schedule run smoothly. From the questions that feel silly, to the facts that don't sit right. For the half-baked ideas you're not ready to share, to the thing that keeps you up at night. You can work it through with Fae. She lives entirely on your device - no company, no contract, no one, just you.

You're safe, when she is surfing.

When you ask Fae to book something, check something, or interact with an online tool - she does it without disclosing who you are. She represents your interests, not your identity. The website sees a request. It doesn't see you.

With permission, not presumption.

Fae won't do anything on your behalf without your permission. When she needs to take an action - sending something, making a booking, using a tool - she shows you exactly what she's about to do and waits. You stay in control. Always.
